Tip of the Week · Apr 2025
MASTERING ANKARA COLOR BLOCKING

Bold color blocking is having its moment in Nigerian fashion. The key is to pair complementary tones — pair cobalt blue Ankara with burnt orange for a head-turning look that respects tradition.

Funmi Adeyemi
Senior Stylist, Lagos
✂️
Designer Tip · Apr 2025
CHOOSING THE RIGHT FABRIC FOR HUMIDITY

Nigeria's tropical climate demands breathable fabrics. Cotton blends and linen-aso-oke hybrids offer both cultural richness and comfort. Avoid synthetic blends in hot weather — they trap heat.

Kelechi Nwosu
Master Tailor, Enugu
👑
Style Guide · Apr 2025
DRESSING FOR ASO-EBI EVENTS IN 2025

Aso-ebi has evolved beyond uniform looks. This season, coordinate with family through color family rather than identical fabric — choose from the same dye lot for harmony with individuality.

Adaeze Obi
Fashion Editor, Abuja
